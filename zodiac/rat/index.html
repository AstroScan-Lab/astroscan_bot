🐀 Rat — Personality & Compatibility

In the Chinese zodiac, the Rat is associated with the Water element. Unlike a Western birth chart, your Chinese zodiac sign is determined purely by your birth year — no birth time or location needed.

Element: Water  |  Birth years: 1924, 1936, 1948, 1960, 1972, 1984, 1996, 2008, 2020

  • Quick-witted and resourceful
  • Sociable and adaptable
  • Practical, good at saving
  • Can seem opportunistic
  • Curious and observant

Per traditional Chinese zodiac compatibility triangles, Rat shares natural affinity with Dragon and Monkey. Rat and Horse sit in directly opposing positions on the 12-year cycle, traditionally considered the most challenging pairing.
