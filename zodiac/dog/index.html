🐕 Dog — Personality & Compatibility

In the Chinese zodiac, the Dog is associated with the Earth element. Unlike a Western birth chart, your Chinese zodiac sign is determined purely by your birth year — no birth time or location needed.

Element: Earth  |  Birth years: 1922, 1934, 1946, 1958, 1970, 1982, 1994, 2006, 2018

  • Loyal and honest
  • Protective of loved ones
  • Strong sense of justice
  • Can be anxious
  • Reliable, steady friend

Per traditional Chinese zodiac compatibility triangles, Dog shares natural affinity with Tiger and Horse. Dog and Dragon sit in directly opposing positions on the 12-year cycle, traditionally considered the most challenging pairing.
