🐇 Rabbit — Personality & Compatibility

In the Chinese zodiac, the Rabbit is associated with the Wood element. Unlike a Western birth chart, your Chinese zodiac sign is determined purely by your birth year — no birth time or location needed.

Element: Wood  |  Birth years: 1927, 1939, 1951, 1963, 1975, 1987, 1999, 2011, 2023

  • Gentle and diplomatic
  • Sensitive and compassionate
  • Values harmony and stability
  • Can be overly cautious
  • Refined taste

Per traditional Chinese zodiac compatibility triangles, Rabbit shares natural affinity with Goat and Pig. Rabbit and Rooster sit in directly opposing positions on the 12-year cycle, traditionally considered the most challenging pairing.
