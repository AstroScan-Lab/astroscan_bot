🐐 Goat — Personality & Compatibility

In the Chinese zodiac, the Goat is associated with the Earth element. Unlike a Western birth chart, your Chinese zodiac sign is determined purely by your birth year — no birth time or location needed.

Element: Earth  |  Birth years: 1919, 1931, 1943, 1955, 1967, 1979, 1991, 2003, 2015

  • Gentle, artistic, and compassionate
  • Calm and resilient
  • Strong sense of empathy
  • Can be prone to worry
  • Creative and imaginative

Per traditional Chinese zodiac compatibility triangles, Goat shares natural affinity with Rabbit and Pig. Goat and Ox sit in directly opposing positions on the 12-year cycle, traditionally considered the most challenging pairing.
