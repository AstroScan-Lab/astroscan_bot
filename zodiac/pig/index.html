🐖 Pig — Personality & Compatibility

In the Chinese zodiac, the Pig is associated with the Water element. Unlike a Western birth chart, your Chinese zodiac sign is determined purely by your birth year — no birth time or location needed.

Element: Water  |  Birth years: 1923, 1935, 1947, 1959, 1971, 1983, 1995, 2007, 2019

  • Warm-hearted and generous
  • Diligent, enjoys comfort
  • Honest and straightforward
  • Can seem naive
  • Sociable, enjoys good company

Per traditional Chinese zodiac compatibility triangles, Pig shares natural affinity with Rabbit and Goat. Pig and Snake sit in directly opposing positions on the 12-year cycle, traditionally considered the most challenging pairing.
