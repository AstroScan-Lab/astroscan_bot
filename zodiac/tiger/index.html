🐅 Tiger — Personality & Compatibility

In the Chinese zodiac, the Tiger is associated with the Wood element. Unlike a Western birth chart, your Chinese zodiac sign is determined purely by your birth year — no birth time or location needed.

Element: Wood  |  Birth years: 1926, 1938, 1950, 1962, 1974, 1986, 1998, 2010, 2022

  • Brave and confident
  • Natural leader, competitive
  • Passionate and dynamic
  • Can be impulsive
  • Independent-minded

Per traditional Chinese zodiac compatibility triangles, Tiger shares natural affinity with Horse and Dog. Tiger and Monkey sit in directly opposing positions on the 12-year cycle, traditionally considered the most challenging pairing.
