🔒 Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 23, 2026

1. Zero-Retention Principle

AstroScan operates on a strict zero-retention policy on our servers. We do not collect, store, or log any personal information entered into the test or the Telegram bot on any server. Birth data is processed ephemerally and permanently discarded upon report generation.

The test website itself saves two small preferences only in your own browser's local storage (your selected language, and an optional mood tracker value) — this data never leaves your device, is never transmitted to us or anyone else, and is fully under your control (clearing your browser data removes it instantly).

2. Telegram Integration

All extended services are provided via @ASTROSCAN_BOT. Payments are processed through Telegram Stars; we do not have access to your payment details. See Telegram's Privacy Policy for more.

3. Analytics

This website uses privacy-friendly analytics (LibreCounter, OpenCounterAPI) that do not use cookies and do not track individuals. Only aggregated, anonymized visit counts are stored.

4. No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer any user data. Since we do not store data, there is nothing to share.

5. Changes

Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

6. Contact

For privacy inquiries: @ASTROSCAN_BOT.
